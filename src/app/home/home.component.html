About Taniti

Taniti is a small tropical island in the Pacific Ocean. Taniti City sits on the white-sand beaches of Yellow Leaf Bay. As you move inland, the island boasts rain forests, mountains, and a volcano ripe for exploring! Taniti has extensive and affordable public transportation to help make it easy to visit the various bars, clubs, and restraunts or see the unique architecture around town. With museums, snokerling, breweries, and zip-lining through the rain forest, theres something for everyone!
