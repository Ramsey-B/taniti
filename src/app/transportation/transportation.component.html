How to explore the island

Tanit prides itself on its public transportation. The public busses serve Taniti City every day from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m, the rest of the island is served by private busses. Taxi cabs are common especially around the Airport, Downtown restraunts, and the beaches. Rental cars are available at the airport. Rental bikes are abundant around the beaches and city. Taniti is also a very flat and walkable city so many vistors elect to stroll amoung our amazing architecture.
