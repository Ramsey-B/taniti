White Sands of Taniti Resort

White Sands is a beautiful four-star resort with a private beach on the northern shores of Yellow Leaf Bay. The resort has 3 large pool areas, 2 spas, next to a 9-hole golf course, boat and jet ski rentals, and a bar and restraunt. With all these activities, we understand you may need a nice place to relax. Thats why our all our rooms are equipped with improved sound-proofing, pillow mattress toppers, spacious bathrooms, ultra wide HD televisions, high-speed Wifi, and a beautiful view of the bay. White Sands is here to help share the beauty of Taniti while providing our guests an upscale environment at an affordable price.
